About us

 Akshaya Agro Food Private Limited is the leading Manufacturer of Turmeric Powder, Handmade Papad. All offered products are processed in the well-equipped environment under the supervision of experts by following norms using pure ingredients. All these products are provided to the customer after tested on various quality parameters. Highly demanded in different industries, these products are accessible from the market in different shapes and sizes.
We make sure that the offered range is packaged with high-grade packing material. These products are processed using advanced processing techniques in compliance with food industry standards. Our quality integrated assortment is the production of the combination of the durable work of our hard-working manpower and the latest farming techniques that we own as our pride. All of our products are enriched with all essential nutrients that are required in a balanced diet.
Working under the leadership of our mentor, Mr. Raj Kumar, We have proved our competency in the market by serving the need of our vast clientele effectively. Their extensive experience and in-depth domain knowledge have helped us to garner a spread out clientele across the nation.
